All files are in the gerber format.

*.drl is the drill file
*.gbl is the Back Copper (also denoted by -B_Cu)
*.gtl is the Front Copper (also denoted by -F_Cu)
*.gbr is the edged Cuts (also denoted by -Edge_Cuts)

Both the Back and Front copper should also have the edge cuts on them.
The board is set to be an 8' by 8" double clad copper board.
